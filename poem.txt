Vanakkam nān ippozhuthu kūrapōvathu  
Bhārathiyār kavithaigaḷil irundhu oru  
Pagudiyāna Pānchāli Sabathathil Draupathi  
Kannanukku seyyum pirārthanai  

Sūdhāṭṭaththil Pāṇḍavargaḷ Draupathiyai vaitthu sūdhādi thōṟrathum,  
Dussāsanan Draupathiyai mānabhangapaduththum pozuthu  
Aval Kannanidam seyyum pirārthanai:  

"Hari Hari Hari enraal Kanna  
Abayam abayam unakku abayam enraal  
Kariyeṉu karul purindhē andru  
Kayaththidai mudhalaiyin uyir maṇiththāy  

Kariya nanniramudaiyāy andru  
Kālingan thalaimisai nadam purindhāy  
Periyadhor porulāvaay Kanna  
Pēsarum pazhamarai porulāvaay  

Chakkaramēndhi ninrāy Kanna  
Chāngam endroru villai karaththudaiyāy  
Akshara porulāvaay Kanna  
Akkāra mamudhuṇṇum pasum kuḻandhāy  

Thukkangaḷ azhiththiduvāy Kanna  
Thoṇḍar kaṇṇīrgaḷai thudaiththiduvāy  
Thakkavar thamai kāppāy andha  
Sathurmuga vēthanai padaiththuvittāy  

Vāṇaththul vāṇāvāy thī maṇ nīr kāṟṟinil avaiyāvāy  
Mōnaththul vīzhndiruppār thavamunivar tham agaththinil uḷir tharuvāy  

Kānatthu poigaile thani kamalamen bhūmisai vīr̤riruppāl  
Srīdhēvi aval thāḷinai kaikondhu magizhndiruppāy  

Ādhiyil āthi appā Kanna  
Aṟivinai kadantha viṇṇagap poruḷē  
Jothikku Jothiyappā enran sollinai kēṭṭaruḷ seydhiduvāy  

Mādhi veliinile naduvānatthi paṟandhidum Garuḍan misai  
Sōdhikkul ūrndhiduvāy Kanna sudar poruḷē pēraḍar poruḷē  

Nampi ninṟu thozhudēn ennai nān azhariyāthingu kāththaruḷuvāy  
Vaiyagam kāththiduvāy Kanna Maṇivaṇṇā  
Enran manachchudarē aiyā nin pathamalare saraṇ  

Hari Hari Hari Hari Hari enraal  
Nandri
